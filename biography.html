Overview

Ugochukwu Jonathan is a Nigerian full-stack web developer, AI product builder, and technology entrepreneur. He is the founder and sole creator of UGO AI Studio, an AI-powered platform focused on automation, digital productivity, and practical software systems for a global audience.

Early Life and Background

He originates from Isi-Uzo Local Government Area of Enugu State, Nigeria. Raised with values centered on faith, discipline, and entrepreneurship, he was influenced by his mother, Favour Chinonso Jonathan, an entrepreneur in the automobile and lubricants industry.
